# Welcome!

This is the first post on my Personal OS - a digital space I've built to share thoughts, showcase work, and experiment with ideas.

## What is Personal OS?

Personal OS is more than just a website. It's a concept: treating your digital presence as an operating system that runs your online life. Just like a computer's OS manages applications and files, Personal OS manages different aspects of your digital identity:

- **Home** - The dashboard, your digital front door
- **Work** - Projects, experiments, and portfolio pieces
- **Writing** - Blog posts, essays, and thoughts
- **About** - The story of who you are

## Built With Modern Tools

This site is built with:

- **Astro** - For blazing-fast static site generation
- **React** - For interactive components when needed
- **TailwindCSS** - For beautiful, consistent styling
- **MDX** - For rich, component-enhanced content
