## About the Job

Amazon S3 (Simple Storage Service) is one of the most widely used cloud storage services in the world, storing trillions of objects and handling millions of requests per second. As a Senior Product Manager - Technical on the S3 team, you will define and drive the product strategy for features that power mission-critical workloads for millions of customers globally.

You will work with world-class engineers and scientists to build scalable, durable, and performant storage solutions that serve as the foundation for countless applications and services.

## Responsibilities

- Define product vision and strategy for Amazon S3 features and capabilities
- Own the end-to-end product lifecycle from ideation through launch and iteration
- Write detailed product requirements documents (PRDs) and work backwards from customer needs
- Partner with engineering teams to prioritize features and drive execution
- Analyze usage patterns and customer feedback to identify opportunities
- Work with cross-functional teams including marketing, sales, and support
- Drive adoption through developer advocacy, documentation, and go-to-market strategy
- Define and track success metrics, iterate based on data-driven insights

## Minimum Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field
- 5+ years of product management experience, with focus on cloud infrastructure or storage systems
- Strong technical background with ability to understand distributed systems architecture
- Experience launching and scaling enterprise-grade products
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally and influence without authority

## Preferred Qualifications

- Master's degree or MBA
- Experience with cloud storage systems, object storage, or file systems
- Deep understanding of AWS services and cloud-native architectures
- Experience with data analytics, machine learning, or big data workloads
- Track record of delivering customer-obsessed products at scale
- Experience working in a fast-paced, high-growth environment

## About Amazon S3

Amazon S3 was launched in 2006 and has grown to become the industry-leading object storage service. S3 is designed for 99.999999999% (11 9's) of durability and stores data for millions of applications. Our customers include enterprises, startups, and developers who rely on S3 for data lakes, backup and restore, archive, IoT, and machine learning workloads.
